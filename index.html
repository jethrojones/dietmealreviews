The scoring system

Five dimensions. No taste scores.

Most food media scores taste. We don't — taste is subjective and can't be standardized. These five dimensions can be.

25% weight
Protein Value
Cost per gram of protein, benchmarked against the meal delivery market
20% weight
Nutritional Accuracy
Proxy from review text analysis + USDA Organic certification bonus
20% weight
Delivery Reliability
Trustpilot rating as a scaled proxy for sustained operational performance
20% weight
Price Transparency
Gap between advertised per-meal price and true all-in cost with shipping
15% weight
Menu Flexibility
Diet variety, skip/cancel policy, à la carte availability, and subscription terms
