How scores are calculated

The methodology behind these rankings

Every score on this page is derived from a fixed formula applied equally to all services. No service can pay to improve its ranking. The composite uses all five dimensions: protein value (25%), nutritional accuracy (20%), delivery reliability (20%), price transparency (20%), and menu flexibility (15%).

Nutritional accuracy is a proxy metric derived from Trustpilot review text analysis (40 reviews per service), not lab-tested macro verification. Zero accuracy-specific complaints were found across all seven services in our June 2026 sample. Services with USDA Organic certification receive a +0.5 bonus reflecting third-party ingredient oversight.

Services marked ⊙ manual seed use nutrition data sourced from each service's published plan pages and help documentation — not individual meal-level scrapes. Their menus require authentication to access programmatically.
